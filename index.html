Official United Membership

Join the Movement with
MUSC Bangalore

Become an Official United Member and be counted as part of the Bangalore supporters club. Here's how to get started on your journey into the Red Army.

01

Buy Your OUM

Purchase your One United Membership from the official Manchester United website. Membership starts at £37.50. Choose Full, Premium, or Junior — not Forwarding memberships.

Forwarding memberships don't count towards supporters' club registration
02

Use a Bangalore Address

Register using a Bangalore address so you're mapped to the city. If your address is from another region, you may need to register with the local supporters club there.

Direct Debit is only available for eligible UK/US bank accounts
03

Fill the MUSCB Form

After buying your OUM, fill out the MUSCB confirmation form. This registers you with our local supporters club and unlocks all local member benefits.

Once confirmed, you're officially one of us
